About AeroBreath Portal

AeroBreath is an advanced, next-generation environmental intelligence platform. It bridges the gap between public respiratory health journaling and real-time ambient air quality metrics.

📊

Real-time Monitoring

Environmental officers manage sensors and log precise AQI, PM2.5, and PM10 metrics across cities.

📓

Health Journaling

Citizens log daily symptoms to construct a personal exposure log and track long-term health anomalies.

🧬

Correlation Analytics

Medical researchers leverage diagnostic tools to trace relationships between air pollution spikes and clinical logs.
